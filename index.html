Entrée

Register visitors who arrive at your building
sending an email with the photograph and information of the visitor to the inviter.

Advantages of using Entrée

When using Entrée these are some of the advantages that you can find.

Easy access

Through our application you can register, take a photo and send it to the inviter, your inviter will come out to find you, which prevents you from wasting time.

Security

Entrée allows you to be more secure, only visitors acompanied by employees can gain acces to the building.

How Entrée works

The Entrée interface has two options, if you are a visitor you can send a notification to your inviter, when you arrive to the building. If you are employee, Entrée can recognize you and register the time access to the building.

I am a visitor

Create an appointment is your goal, first select the visitor option, then fill the form with your personal information, choose who is your host, the reason of your visit and most importantly do not miss it, click on the camera button, smile and take your picture. send your appointment and wait your host will be notified :) your host already come out for you.

I am a student

Select the student option, take a picture, Entree can recognize you and register your time access, this is an useful information for staff
